## RECORDING TIPS:

✅ **DO:**
- Speak clearly and conversationally (like explaining to a friend)
- Show the tool actually working (even if it's fast)
- Smile (especially in selfie videos)
- Practice the script 2-3 times before recording
- Record in a quiet room
- Use good lighting (face a window)

❌ **DON'T:**
- Read the script robotically
- Go over 2 minutes (judges will stop watching)
- Use filler words ("um," "like," "you know")
- Apologize or downplay your work
- Record in a noisy environment
