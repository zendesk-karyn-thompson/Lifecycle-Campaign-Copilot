What is Pre-MQL Nurture?

Pre-MQL nurture is an always-on program that keeps leads warm between your campaigns. It runs year-round, delivering relevant content to help leads move through their buyer journey—without overwhelming them.

Support the Buyer Journey

❤️

Shorten Time to MQL

Keep nurture running alongside campaigns to accelerate the journey

What Does Success Look Like?

Here's the truth: Nurture success is NOT about MQLs. It's about creating engaged web sessions that signal genuine interest.

Primary Success Signal

👁️ Engaged Web Sessions

An engaged web session means a lead visited your site and took meaningful actions—like viewing multiple pages, exploring products, or reading resources. This shows real intent, not just a random click.

Metric What It Tells Us How Nurture Drives It
✅ Engaged Web Sessions Real interest and intent. Lead is exploring and learning. Nurture emails drive qualified traffic to relevant content
✅ Web Sessions to Trial Lead is ready to experience the product hands-on Quality nurture content builds confidence to try
📊 Opens & Clicks Directional signal, but doesn't affect lead score Useful for optimization, not the goal
✅ MQLs & Pipeline Supporting outcomes—nurture contributes but isn't the primary driver Nurture keeps leads warm so campaigns can convert

Key Principles

Always-On

Nurture runs 24/7/365. It never stops keeping leads warm, even when you're not running campaigns.

Less Noise, More Reach

Send less email per person, but reach more unique people. Quality over quantity.

Behavior-Driven

Content adapts based on what leads do, not just who they are. Actions drive the journey.
